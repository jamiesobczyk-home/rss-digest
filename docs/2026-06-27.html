IBM MCGA Gate Array Reverse Engineering

IBM MCGA, a low-cost video chipset used in PS/2 models 25 and 30, consists of two gate arrays that have been reverse engineered with complete schematics and layouts now available. The reverse engineering uncovered undocumented features including genlock capability for external sync signals and selectable clock frequency modes. Astronomers have discovered two Jupiter-sized exoplanets 1,110 light-years away that are the least dense of their size, with densities lower than cotton candy, likely composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.
