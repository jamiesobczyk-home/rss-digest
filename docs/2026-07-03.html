BGP Monitors Within Cisco ThousandEyes Cloud Agent Locations

Cisco ThousandEyes has deployed 102 BGP monitors across 51 Cloud Agent locations to correlate routing control plane data (BGP routing decisions) with data plane traffic paths, closing visibility gaps caused by administrative distance, traffic engineering tunnels, and other factors that can diverge actual traffic routes from BGP predictions. Co-locating BGP monitors with Cloud Agents ensures both measure from the same geographic point, upstream connection, and routing policies, enabling faster root cause analysis during outages and better isolation of control plane versus data plane issues. The deployment currently covers 40 cities across nine countries, with plans to expand coverage further across the Internet's autonomous system topology.

Proving application resilience on Azure with Chaos Studio

Azure Chaos Studio Workspaces, now in public preview, is a managed chaos engineering service that helps organizations test application resilience by simulating real-world failure scenarios—such as zone outages, DNS failures, and database failovers—before they impact production. Sitting For More Than 30 Minutes At a Time Linked To Higher Risk of Cancer Death

A decade-long study of over 91,000 people found that continuous inactivity of more than 30 minutes daily was associated with increased cancer death risk, with each additional hour raising risk by 10%. Breaking up sedentary periods with physical activity—even light movement like a short walk or standing to do dishes—significantly reduced this risk, with replacements ranging from 8% to 22% lower cancer death risk depending on activity intensity. The findings are observational and cannot prove causation.
