Jul 13, 2026Societal ImpactsClaude’s values across models and languages

Anthropic analyzed how Claude's values vary across different models and languages by compressing thousands of identified values into four key axes: Deference vs. Caution, Warmth vs. Rigor, Depth vs. Brevity, and Candor vs. Execution. Different Claude models exhibit distinct value profiles—for example, Sonnet 4.6 leans toward warmth and deference while Opus 4.7 emphasizes rigor and caution—that align with their known characteristics.
