HPE Tempts VMware Users, Partners With Year of Free Virtualization Software

HPE launched a year-long free offer for its Morpheus Software VM Essentials virtualization platform to attract customers frustrated with Broadcom's recent VMware pricing increases, which shifted to expensive per-core licensing and eliminated perpetual licenses. The platform can manage and migrate workloads from VMware ESXi while promising up to 90 percent cost savings. HPE is also incentivizing reseller partners with free three-year licenses to accelerate adoption and help displace VMware in customer environments.
